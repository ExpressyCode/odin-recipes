Carnivore Ice Cream

Description

A rich and creamy ice cream made with just heavy cream, egg yolks, and honey.

Ingredients

  • 4 raw egg yolks
  • 5 dl heavy whipping cream
  • 60g of honey
  • Pinch of sea salt

Steps

  1. Separate the egg yolks from the egg whites.
  2. In a bowl whisk egg yolks, heavy whipping cream, honey and sea salt
  3. Put the mix in the freeze for 4 - 6 hours and enjoy.
