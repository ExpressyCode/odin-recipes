Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled egg is very healthy and easy to make. It doesn't require a lot of your time to make either.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 eggs
  • 1 dl of whole milk
  • butter
  • salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the pan.
  2. Mix the eggs, whole milk, salt and black pepper in a bowl.
  3. Add the butter to the pan and let it melt.
  4. Add the mix from step 2.
  5. Stir the mix in the pan until it becomes firm to your taste.
  6. Put the scrambled eggs on a plate and enjoy. If you want, you can put a little butter on top of the scrambled eggs and add spices to your liking.
